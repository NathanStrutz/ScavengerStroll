Scavenger Stroll

How To Play

Thanks for checking out our Scavenger Stroll! This game is simple and fun to play. All you have to do is choose one of the scavenger hunts. On each scavenger hunt there are ten items. Find all ten items in Wannamaker County Park, and you win! Please select either the Park scavenger hunt or the Nature scavenger hunt to begin the fun!

Information about FLL team The Quad Pod

We are an FLL (FIRST Lego League) team, number 50751, The Quad Pod. Each year for FLL we build a robot, solve missions, and come up with a solution to a challenge. The 2020-2021 FIRST FLL season, RePLAY, challenges all teams to create a project that gets people outside and more active. Our project is called Scavenger Stroll (the website you are on now), and its purpose is to help people enjoy being outside and more active by looking for items on the scavenger list.
